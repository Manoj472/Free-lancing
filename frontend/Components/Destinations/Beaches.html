World's Most Beautiful Beaches

Whitehaven Beach

Located in Australia, Whitehaven is famous for its crystal-clear waters and pristine white sands.

Navagio Beach

A secluded beach in Greece known for its dramatic cliffs and turquoise waters, accessible only by boat.

Anse Source d’Argent

Located in the Seychelles, this beach is known for its pink sand, granite boulders, and shallow waters.
