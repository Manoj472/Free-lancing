Vizag - The City of Destiny

The Jewel of Andhra Pradesh

Visakhapatnam, commonly known as Vizag, is the largest city and financial capital of Andhra Pradesh. Nestled between the Eastern Ghats and the Bay of Bengal, Vizag is famous for its picturesque beaches, serene landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.

  • Home to India's first submarine museum and the famous INS Kursura
  • Features the longest coastline in the state with pristine beaches
  • Boasts lush green hills like Kailasagiri and picturesque Araku Valley
  • Major industrial hub with India's largest port
  • Known for its delicious seafood and unique Andhra cuisine

Often called the "Goa of the East Coast," Vizag is a perfect blend of natural beauty, modern infrastructure, and historic significance. The city offers everything from ancient Buddhist sites to high-tech IT parks, making it truly worthy of its nickname "The City of Destiny."
